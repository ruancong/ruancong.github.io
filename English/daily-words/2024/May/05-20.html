中文释义

v. 指挥；引导；管理；举止；传导 n. 行为；举止；品行

辅助记忆

记忆方法

  1. 方法一：想象一位指挥家在指挥乐团，展现"conduct"作为动词的含义。🎭
  2. 方法二：联想"con-"(一起) + "duct"(引导)，表示"引导、管理"。
  3. 方法三：把"conduct"看作"con"(共同) + "duct"(管道)，表示"引导行为"。
  4. 场景记忆法：记住老师在课堂上维持秩序的场景。👨‍🏫
  5. 动作记忆法：模仿指挥家挥动指挥棒的动作。🎵
  6. 情感联结法：联想到自己表现良好时获得的表扬。👍
  7. 分组分类法：与"behavior"、"manner"等词分组记忆。

词根词源

  • 来自拉丁语 "conductus"，意为"引导、带领"
  • "con-" 表示"一起"，"duct" 表示"引导、带领"

常用词组

词组使用场景说明
conduct business经营业务
conduct research进行研究
conduct oneself举止行为
code of conduct行为准则

联想记忆

  • 近义词：behavior, manner, management
  • 反义词：misconduct, misbehavior
  • 联想词：direct, guide, lead
  • 同根词：conductor, conduction
  • 形近词：product, conduit

台词记忆

"Your conduct in this matter has been exemplary." - 常见商务用语

"你在这件事上的表现很出色。"

结合记忆宫殿与地点

  1. 地点记忆词："音乐厅"
    • "The conductor conducts the orchestra."
    • "指挥家指挥管弦乐队。"
  2. 地点记忆词："教室"
    • "Students should conduct themselves properly."
    • "学生应该举止得体。"
  3. 地点记忆词："实验室"
    • "Scientists conduct experiments carefully."
    • "科学家谨慎地进行实验。"

造句

  1. The professor conducts research on climate change. 教授进行气候变化研究。
  2. Everyone must follow the company's code of conduct. 每个人都必须遵守公司的行为准则。
  3. She always conducts herself with dignity and grace. 她总是举止优雅得体。

中文释义

adj. 价格适中的；经济实惠的；负担得起的

辅助记忆

记忆方法

  1. 方法一：联想"afford"(负担得起) + "-able"，表示"能够负担得起"。💵
  2. 方法二：想象一个人能够负担得起日常开销的场景。
  3. 方法三：联想"afford"的意思，加上"-able"就是"经济实惠的"。
  4. 场景记忆法：想象一个人在旅游时选择经济实惠的住宿的场景。🏨
  5. 动作记忆法：用手做出负担得起的动作。🙆
  6. 情感联结法：回忆自己在经济实惠的情况下感到的满足。😌
  7. 分组分类法：与"reasonable"、"moderate"等词分组记忆。

词根词源

  • 来自拉丁语 "afferre"，意为"带来、带去"
  • "af-"(带来) + "fordere"(要求)

常用词组

词组使用场景说明

联想记忆

  • 近义词：reasonable, moderate, economical
  • 反义词：expensive, costly, unaffordable
  • 联想词：budget, bargain, discount
  • 同根词：afford, affordable
  • 形近词：affordability, affordably

台词记忆

"我们为低收入家庭提供经济实惠的住房选择。"

  1. 地点记忆词："住宅区"
    • "The neighborhood has many affordable housing options."
    • "这个社区有很多经济实惠的住房选择。"
  2. 地点记忆词："超市"
    • "The supermarket offers affordable prices on fresh produce."
    • "这家超市在新鲜蔬菜上有经济实惠的价格。"
  3. 地点记忆词："医院"
    • "The hospital provides affordable care for uninsured patients."
    • "这家医院为没有保险的患者提供经济实惠的照顾。"

造句

  1. She found an affordable apartment near her workplace. 她在工作场所附近找到了一套经济实惠的公寓。
  2. The company offers affordable insurance plans for its employees. 这家公司为员工提供经济实惠的保险计划。
  3. The government is working on making healthcare more affordable. 政府正在努力使医疗保健更加经济实惠。

中文释义

adj. 全能的；无所不能的；万能的

辅助记忆

记忆方法

  1. 方法一：联想"omni-"(全部) + "potent"(有能力)，表示"全部有能力"。🌟
  2. 方法二：想象一个人在各个领域都有出色表现的场景。
  3. 方法三：联想"potent"的意思，加上"omni-"就是"全能的"。
  4. 场景记忆法：想象一个人在工作、学业和社交中都取得成功的场景。🌈
  5. 动作记忆法：用手做出全能的动作。💥
  6. 情感联结法：回忆自己在面对挑战时的自信和信心。💡
  7. 分组分类法：与"all-powerful"、"almighty"等词分组记忆。

词根词源

  • 来自拉丁语 "omnipotens"，意为"全能的"
  • "omni-"(全部) + "potens"(有能力)

常用词组

词组使用场景说明
omnipotent god全能的神
omnipotent entity全能的实体
omnipotent leader全能的领导
omnipotent force全能的力量

联想记忆

  • 近义词：all-powerful, almighty, supreme
  • 反义词：powerless, weak, helpless
  • 联想词：authority, control, dominance
  • 同根词：omnipresent, omniscient
  • 形近词：omnipotence, omnipotency

台词记忆

"Some people believe in the existence of an omnipotent being." - 常见讨论话题

"有些人相信存在一个全能的存在。"

结合记忆宫殿与地点

    • "古代人崇拜着一个全能的神。"
  2. 地点记忆词："政府大楼"
    • "The government is considered an omnipotent entity in some countries."
    • "在某些国家，政府被视为一个全能的实体。"
  3. 地点记忆词："体育场"
    • "He is known as an omnipotent leader in the sports world."
    • "他在体育界被称为一个全能的领导者。"

造句

  1. Some people believe that artificial intelligence will eventually become omnipotent. 有些人相信人工智能最终会变得全能。
  2. He was seen as an omnipotent force in the political arena. 他在政治领域被视为一个全能的力量。
  3. The company's new product is expected to revolutionize the industry and make it omnipotent. 这家公司的新产品预计将彻底颠覆这个行业并使其变得全能。

单词文章

Hunter John set out early one cold morning for his daily patrol. His task was to monitor the conduct around the village, ensuring that no illegal activities were taking place. He knew his job was important because any improper conduct could lead to serious consequences.

Recently, a problem had arisen in the village: many young people were struggling with an addiction to a new drug. This addiction not only made it difficult for them to work but also caused a lot of distress for their families. To address this issue, John and the other villagers decided to take action to transfer the young people's attention away from the drug and help them overcome their addiction.

The village had limited resources, and the quota for supplies was tight. Therefore, everyone had to use resources sparingly and avoid waste. Despite the insufficient resources, everyone worked together, hoping to inhibit the spread of this addiction.

After many discussions, the villagers decided to suspend all unnecessary activities temporarily and focus all their efforts on this issue. They also came up with some affordable entertainment options, hoping to attract the young people and help them find new interests.

John knew that solving this problem would take time and patience, but he believed that if everyone worked together, they could overcome even the seemingly omnipotent challenge. Eventually, their village would return to peace, and everyone could live happily.
